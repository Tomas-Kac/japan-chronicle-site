Welcome to Japan Chronicle

An interactive atlas of premodern Japan, 552–1869 — every battle, event and figure on one timeline.

  • Drag the year slider at the bottom to move through history, era by era.
  • Toggle layers (top‑right): castles, march routes, events, people, provinces and domains.
  • Click any marker — a battle, a castle, a person — to read its story.
  • Search (top) for a battle, clan, commander or place.
